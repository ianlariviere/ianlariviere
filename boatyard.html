Working at the boat yard

After high school, I worked at my family's small business- a boat yard in Downeast Maine. We haul, launch, maintain, and store boats. We also set docks and moorings. Over the year, I learned and practiced a wide variety of skills.

  • Sanding, varnishing, painting, polishing, waxing
  • Operating the fork truck, tractor, and hydraulic trailer to pick up and move boats
  • Maneuvering/towing boats in the water for delivery
  • Using a bosun's chair to make repairs to masts
  • Splicing rope
  • Stepping masts and rigging sails
  • Communicating effectively with customers
